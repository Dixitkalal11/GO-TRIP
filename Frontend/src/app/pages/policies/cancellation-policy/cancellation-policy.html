1. General Cancellation Policy

GoTrip understands that travel plans can change. This Cancellation Policy outlines the terms and conditions for canceling bookings and obtaining refunds. Please read this policy carefully before making a booking.

2. Cancellation Policy for All Bookings

GoTrip applies a uniform cancellation policy across all booking types (Bus, Train, and Flight):

2.1 Cancellation Fee

  • Cancellation Fee: 10% of ticket price
  • Maximum Fee: ₹500 (even if 10% exceeds ₹500)
  • Refund Amount: Ticket price minus cancellation fee

Example: If you cancel a ticket worth ₹1,000, the cancellation fee is ₹100 (10% of ₹1,000), and you receive ₹900 as refund. If you cancel a ticket worth ₹10,000, the cancellation fee is ₹500 (capped at maximum), and you receive ₹9,500 as refund.

2.2 Eligibility for Cancellation

  • Only confirmed bookings can be cancelled
  • Pending bookings cannot be cancelled (they can be abandoned)
  • Cancelled bookings cannot be cancelled again

5. How to Cancel

  • Log in to your GoTrip account
  • Go to "My Bookings" section
  • Select the booking you wish to cancel
  • Click on "Cancel Booking" and follow the instructions
  • You can also cancel via our customer support: +91 123 456 7890

6. Refund Processing

6.1 Refund Timeline

  • Processing Time: Refunds are processed within 10 business days from the date of cancellation
  • Refund status will be updated in your transaction history
  • You will receive email confirmation once refund is processed

6.2 Refund Method

  • Refunds are processed to the original payment method used for booking
  • Refund amount = Ticket price - Cancellation fee (10% of price, max ₹500)
  • Refund appears in your account based on your payment gateway's processing time

6.3 Coin Refund

  • Coins Used: If you used coins for the booking, they will be refunded to your account immediately upon cancellation
  • Coins Earned: Coins earned on the cancelled booking will be deducted from your account
  • Coin refunds are processed automatically and appear in your coin balance

7. Special Circumstances

7.1 Service Provider Cancellation

If the service provider (bus operator, airline, railway) cancels the service:

  • Full refund will be processed automatically
  • No cancellation charges apply
  • You will be notified via email and SMS

7.2 Medical Emergencies

In case of medical emergencies, please contact our customer support with:

  • Medical certificate from a registered doctor
  • Booking reference number
  • Supporting documents

We will review your case and may provide full or partial refund at our discretion.

8. Partial Cancellation

  • For group bookings, partial cancellation may be allowed
  • Cancellation charges apply only to cancelled tickets
  • Remaining passengers can travel as per original booking

9. Date Change/Rescheduling

  • Date changes are subject to availability and fare difference
  • Rescheduling charges may apply
  • Some tickets may not be eligible for date change
  • Please contact customer support for rescheduling requests

10. No-Show Policy

  • If you do not show up for your booked service, no refund will be provided
  • Please cancel your booking in advance to avoid no-show charges
  • In case of genuine emergencies, contact our support team

11. Cancellation Process

11.1 How to Cancel

  1. Log in to your GoTrip account
  2. Navigate to "My Bookings" section
  3. Find the booking you wish to cancel (must be in "confirmed" status)
  4. Click on "Cancel Ticket" button
  5. Provide cancellation reason (optional)
  6. Confirm the cancellation

11.2 What Happens After Cancellation

  • Booking status changes to "cancelled"
  • Cancellation fee transaction is recorded
  • Refund transaction is created (status: pending)
  • Coins used are refunded to your account
  • Coins earned are deducted from your account
  • You receive an email confirmation with cancellation details

  • Live Chat: Available on our website
