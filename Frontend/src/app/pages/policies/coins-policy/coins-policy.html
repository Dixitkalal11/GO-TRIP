1. Introduction to GoTrip Coins

GoTrip Coins is our loyalty rewards program that allows you to earn coins on bookings and redeem them for discounts on future travel. This policy explains how the coins system works, how to earn and redeem coins, and the terms and conditions governing their use.

2. How Coins Work

  • Earning Coins: You earn coins when you make successful bookings through GoTrip
  • Coin Value: 1 GoTrip Coin = ₹1 (1 coin equals 1 Indian Rupee discount)
  • Redemption: Coins can be used to get discounts on future bookings
  • Progressive System: Maximum coins you can use per booking increases with your booking history
  • Non-Transferable: Coins are linked to your account and cannot be transferred

3. How to Earn Coins

3.1 Booking Rewards

You earn coins on every successful booking:

  • All Bookings: Earn 2% of your booking amount as coins
  • Maximum Per Booking: Up to 50 coins per booking (capped at ₹2,500 booking value)
  • Coins Credited: Coins are added to your account immediately after successful payment

Example: If you book a ticket worth ₹1,000, you earn 20 coins (2% of ₹1,000). If you book a ticket worth ₹3,000, you earn 50 coins (capped at maximum).

3.2 Discount Type Bonuses

When you use special discount types, you can earn additional coins:

  • Regular Booking: Base 10 coins + progressive bonus
  • Student Discount (15% off): Base 25 coins + progressive bonus (requires 50 coins to unlock)
  • Senior Citizen Discount (20% off, max ₹600): Base 30 coins + progressive bonus (requires 100 coins to unlock)

3.3 Progressive Bonus System

Your coin earnings increase based on your booking history:

  • First 3 Bookings: Base coins + 5 bonus coins
  • 4th Booking Onwards: Base coins + 3 bonus coins

Example: On your 1st booking with Regular discount, you earn 15 coins (10 base + 5 bonus). On your 5th booking, you earn 13 coins (10 base + 3 bonus).

4. How to Redeem Coins

4.1 Redemption Rules

  • Coin Value: 1 coin = ₹1 discount
  • Maximum Coins Per Booking: Based on your booking history (progressive system):
    • 1st Booking: Maximum 5 coins (₹5 discount)
    • 2nd Booking: Maximum 10 coins (₹10 discount)
    • 3rd Booking: Maximum 15 coins (₹15 discount)
    • 4th+ Bookings: 15 + (booking count - 2) × 3 coins
  • Actual Maximum: The lower of:
    • Your available coin balance
    • Maximum coins allowed per trip (based on booking history)
    • 50% of booking amount (in coins)
  • Partial Redemption: You can use any amount up to the maximum allowed
  • Combination: Coins can be used with other discounts and offers

4.2 How to Use Coins

  1. Proceed to the payment page after selecting your booking
  2. You'll see your available coin balance and maximum usable coins
  3. Toggle "Use GoTrip Coins" to enable coin usage
  4. Adjust the number of coins to use (you can use 0%, 25%, 50%, or 100% of maximum)
  5. The discount amount will be displayed in real-time
  6. Complete the payment for the remaining amount after coin discount
  7. Coins are deducted from your account and new coins are earned on the booking amount

5. Coin Balance and Tracking

  • View Balance: Your coin balance is displayed in the navbar and payment page
  • Real-Time Updates: Coin balance updates immediately after booking completion
  • Transaction History: Track coin earnings and usage in your account
  • Net Coin Change: After each booking, your balance changes by: (Coins Earned - Coins Used)

6. Cancellation and Refund Policy for Coin Transactions

6.1 If You Cancel a Booking

  • Coins used for the booking will be refunded to your account
  • Coins earned on that booking will be deducted from your balance
  • Processing time: 24-48 hours after cancellation
  • Refund is processed automatically when cancellation is confirmed

6.2 If Service Provider Cancels

  • Full refund of coins used for the booking
  • Coins earned on that booking will be deducted
  • Your coin balance will be restored to pre-booking state

7. Restrictions and Limitations

  • Coins cannot be converted to cash or withdrawn
  • Coins are non-transferable between accounts
  • Coins cannot be sold, exchanged, or gifted
  • Maximum coins per booking is limited by your booking history (progressive system)
  • Maximum coins cannot exceed 50% of booking amount
  • Coins must be used in whole numbers (no fractional coins)
  • Coins are deducted at the time of booking and cannot be reversed except in case of cancellation

8. Fraud and Misuse

GoTrip reserves the right to:

  • Cancel or suspend coin accounts in case of fraudulent activity
  • Deduct coins earned through fraudulent means or invalid bookings
  • Take legal action against misuse of the coins system
  • Modify or terminate the coins program at any time with notice

9. Account Closure

  • If you close your GoTrip account, all unused coins will be forfeited
  • Coins cannot be transferred or refunded upon account closure
  • Please use your coins before closing your account

10. Program Modifications

GoTrip reserves the right to:

  • Modify coin earning rates and percentages
  • Change maximum coins per booking limits
  • Update progressive bonus system
  • Modify discount type requirements
  • Discontinue the coins program with 30 days' notice

Existing coins in your account will be honored according to current terms.

11. Examples

Example 1: Earning Coins (First Booking)

You book a bus ticket for ₹1,000:

  • Coins earned: 2% of ₹1,000 = 20 coins
  • Maximum coins you can use on next booking: 5 coins (₹5 discount)
  • Your coin balance increases by 20 coins

Example 2: Using Coins (Second Booking)

You have 20 coins and book a train ticket for ₹2,000:

  • Maximum coins allowed: 10 coins (2nd booking limit)
  • You use 10 coins = ₹10 discount
  • Amount to pay: ₹2,000 - ₹10 = ₹1,990
  • Coins earned on this booking: 2% of ₹1,990 = 39.8 coins (rounded to 40 coins)
  • Net coin change: 40 earned - 10 used = +30 coins
  • New balance: 20 + 30 = 50 coins

Example 3: Progressive System (5th Booking)

You have 100 coins and book with Senior Citizen discount (requires 100 coins):

  • Maximum coins allowed: 15 + (5-2) × 3 = 24 coins
  • You use 20 coins = ₹20 discount
  • Senior discount: 20% off (capped at ₹600)
  • Base coins earned: 30 coins
  • Progressive bonus: +3 coins (4th+ booking)
  • Total coins earned: 33 coins
  • Net coin change: 33 earned - 20 used = +13 coins
